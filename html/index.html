About Us

Storyline

The story takes place on Titan, a faraway planet. Players dive into a big battle between good and bad. They play as Tiny Fang, a brave superhero with amazing powers. Tiny Fang has to go on a risky mission to stop The Largest Blade, a strong villain. The bad guy wants to ruin Tiny Fang's plans and cause trouble in the world. Earth's future is at risk, so players have to help Tiny Fang escape from The Largest Blade. They must finish their mission, no matter what.

Description

In "Tiny Fang's Mission," players become a superhero named Tiny Fang. He's on a secret mission to save the world on Titan, a planet far away. But there's a problem! A bad guy called The Largest Blade finds out and tries to stop Tiny Fang.

His important job is to save Earth from being destroyed. Players help Tiny Fang escape from The Largest Blade. It's an exciting adventure full of hiding and using skills.
